Our Mission

To provide the children of Shaare Tefila with: A familiarity and knowledge of Jewish traditions, Love and respect for Torah, Connection to the people of and the land of Israel, An understanding of Jewish history, and Fostering a sense of responsibility to our world through Derech Eretz (ethical decency) and Tikkun Olam.

Core Values

    • KEHILAH KEDOSHAH – We are a “holy community”
      SHALOM BAYIT – “Peace in the house”
      KAVOD – “Respect” or “Honor”
      CHESED – “Kindness” or “Loving kindness”
